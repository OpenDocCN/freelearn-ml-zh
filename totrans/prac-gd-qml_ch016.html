Part III
A Match Made in Heaven: Quantum Machine Learning

This part is devoted to studying different quantum machine learning models. You will learn how to implement and run Quantum Support Vector Machines and Quantum Neural Networks, and how to combine them with classical models to obtain interesting architectures, such as Quantum Generative Adversarial Networks.

This part includes the following chapters:

  • Chapter 8, What is Quantum Machine Learning?

  • Chapter 9, Quantum Support Vector Machines

  • Chapter 10, Quantum Neural Networks

  • Chapter 11, The Best of Both Worlds: Hybrid Architectures

  • Chapter 12, Quantum Generative Adversarial Networks
