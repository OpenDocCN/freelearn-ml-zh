Section 3: Dealing with Big Data, HPC , IoT, and Coud Application Scalability through ML

The new vSphere Scale-Out edition is specifically designed for running high-performance computing (HPC) and big data workloads. We have to ensure that HPC and big data performance and other requirements can be met either by development or by fine-tuning guidelines with our mainstream products. This vSphere edition comes with a feature set that has been adjusted to align with these two Scale-Out workload types, HPC and big data, and at a competitive cost. It packages all the core vSphere features required for big data and HPC workloads and is sold in packs of 8 CPUs. Key features included in the vSphere Scale-Out edition include ESXi hypervisor, vMotion, Storage vMotion, Host Profiles, Auto Deploy, and Distributed Switch.
