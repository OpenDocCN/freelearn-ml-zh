Appendix A. LUIS Entities

In this appendix, we will list the prebuilt entities in LUIS.

LUIS prebuilt entities

The following list shows all the available entities that can be added to your application:

  • DatetimeV2
  • Datetime
  • Number
  • Ordinal
  • Percentage
  • Temperature
  • Dimension
  • Money
  • Age
  • Geography
  • Encyclopedia
  • URL
  • Email
  • Phone Number

A complete and updated list of prebuilt entities can be found at https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cognitive-services/LUIS/pre-builtentities.
